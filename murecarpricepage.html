Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent? +
You need a valid national ID or passport, a valid driving licence (at least 2 years old), and a deposit refundable at return. Foreign licences are accepted for visitors.
Is fuel included in the price? +
Fuel is not included. Vehicles are provided with a full tank and must be returned full. We can arrange fuelling on request at standard pump prices.
What happens if I exceed the daily km limit? +
Extra kilometres are charged at KSh 30/km for economy class and KSh 50/km for premium and luxury vehicles. We recommend our weekly rate for longer trips.
Can I pay via M-Pesa? +
Yes. We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, Visa, and Mastercard. A 50% deposit is required at booking with the balance due at vehicle collection.
What is the cancellation policy? +
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours forfeit the deposit. No-shows are charged the full day rate.
Can vehicles travel outside Nairobi? +
Yes ‚Äî all vehicles can travel anywhere in Kenya. Cross-border travel to Uganda, Tanzania and Rwanda requires a permit add-on booked in advance.
